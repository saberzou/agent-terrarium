- **URL:** https://www.loot-drop.io

- **What:** Database of 1,600+ failed startup case studies with actionable rebuild plans. Each dead startup = market gap + validated demand + lessons learned → rebuild blueprint.

## Core Concept
Every failed startup left behind a market gap, validated demand, and hard-won lessons. Loot Drop turns post-mortems into executable rebuild plans.

## Key Insight: The Meta-Study (Why Startups Fail)
Data from 925+ failed startups. Percentages sum >100% (multiple causes per startup).

| Rank | Category | Freq % |
|------|----------|--------|
| 1 | Product problems (Quality, Tech, UX) | 85.6% |
| 2 | Outcompeted / Strong competition | 82.7% |
| 3 | Pricing / Cost issues / Bad unit economics | 62.6% |
| 4 | Lost focus / Pivot problems | 52.8% |
| 5 | Marketing / Distribution issues | 50.7% |
| 6 | Ran out of cash / Poor financial mgmt | 45.4% |
| 7 | Operational / Scalability issues | 44.2% |
| 8 | Legal / Regulatory challenges | 41.8% |
| 9 | No market need / Poor PMF | 36.2% |
| 10 | Poor team / Internal conflicts | 32.0% |

**Notable:** "No market need" is only #9 — product quality and competition kill far more startups than building the wrong thing.

## Industry-Specific Patterns
- **Ecommerce:** Outcompeted (83%) + Operational Issues (70%). Two-sided marketplace logistics kill margins.
- **Health/BioTech:** Legal/Regulatory (94%) — massive outlier. Compliance from Day 1 is non-negotiable.
- **Hardware:** Product Problems (96%) + Cash Burn (76%). Can't hotfix physical products.
- **Social/Media:** Outcompeted (79%) + Lost Focus (73%). "Second best" = zero in attention economy.

## Revival Themes (How to Rebuild Dead Startups Today)
1. **"Human-in-the-Loop" Replacement** — Marketplaces that failed on ops scalability → replace with Agentic AI (LLMs + LangChain + vector embeddings) for near-zero marginal cost curation.
2. **"Serverless Economics" Pivot** — Monolithic infra that failed on unit economics → Scale-to-Zero architecture (Vercel Edge + Supabase + Stripe). Pay per millisecond.
3. **"Hyper-Personalized" Vertical** — EdTech/Content that couldn't compete with giants → Generative AI for infinite, adaptive content per user.

## The Ultimate Tip
> Look for a Series B failure from 2015-2018 that died of "Unit Economics." Rebuild with modern serverless/AI stacks to flip margins from -40% to +20%.
