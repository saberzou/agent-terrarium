## What happened

- Quiz was floor 10 in swipe system — touch conflicts killed tap events on iOS

- Vocab word highlighting: changed from dotted underline to blue color
- Tried pink/red/lavender palette (#cf151d etc) — Saber said "looks dirty", reverted
- Final accent color: **#fa5860** (coral red) on warm cream backgrounds

## Lessons
- **Revert > fix-forward** when color experiments fail — `git revert --no-edit HEAD` is fast
- **Fluent Emoji** are great placeholder stickers — consistent style, free, swap-friendly
- **touch-action CSS > JS preventDefault** for controlling touch on iOS
