## Summary
A color palette inspiration site by Mackenzie Child that shows palettes applied to a real website layout — not just swatches. Each palette maps colors to specific UI roles (background, headline, button, link, card).

## Notes
- 17 curated palettes ranging from light/warm to dark/moody
- Key insight: colors are assigned **roles**, not just picked for aesthetics
- Every palette includes: background, bg-alt, headline, paragraph, button, link, card-bg, stroke, highlight, secondary, tertiary
- Great for quick theming — copy CSS variables, instant cohesive design
- Already integrated into our `web-craft` skill as `references/color-palettes.md`
